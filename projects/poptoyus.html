POPTOY

April 2, 2025

Over Spring Break, I had the opportunity to make a website for a toy company, POPTOY.

Their old website was outdated and many of the links were broken. They hired freelancers to make their page, and I felt that many of the descriptions were AI generated and insincere.

This is where I could help, I offered to overhaul the whole site to simplify it and update with new products, and give descriptions that are genuine. I also added my own drawings in ways I saw fit.

I learned:

  • Domain transfering--from their old website which was on HostGator to the new one.

  • Building a working Contact-Us form submissions

  • Implementing animations and transitions to make the site interactive and interesting

  • To use @media to design a mobile friendly viewing experience

  • Making a mock up in figma before implementing the HTML/CSS
